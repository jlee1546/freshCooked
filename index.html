Fresh Cooked Wings!

What this is all about!

Do you get as excited as I do when you here, "fresh cooked chicken wings?" Well, that is what this site is all about. I decided to create a website where us "fresh wing lovers" could come together and share tips and information all centered around finding the freshest wings in the area.

Nowadays, companies only care about profit and serving as many wings as possible. In order to speed up the process, many places "par cook" their wings. Par cooking is a process by which the wings are cooked a first time to almost complete doneness. Then, the are refrigerated and held cold until ready to be cooked. A customer orders wings and gets them pretty fast.
